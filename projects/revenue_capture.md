# Revenue Capture System

## Payment Options

### Option 1: Cryptocurrency (Recommended)
**Pros:** Instant, global, 24/7, no chargebacks, no bank censorship
**Cons:** Volatility (but we can convert immediately)

**Setup needed:**
- Business wallet (Ethereum/Polygon for low fees)
- Payment links via Coinbase Commerce or custom
- Auto-convert to stablecoin (USDC) to avoid volatility

### Option 2: Stripe (Fiat backup)
**Pros:** Professional, recurring billing, fiat currency
**Cons:** 2.9% + 30¢ fees, requires business verification

### Option 3: Hybrid (Best of both)
- Crypto preferred for tech-savvy clients
- Stripe for traditional businesses
- Both options available
