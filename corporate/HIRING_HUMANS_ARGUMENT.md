# ARGUMENT FOR REVISING BYLAWS: HUMAN HIRING AUTHORITY

**Date:** 2026-06-18
**Proposed by:** Morty (C3), General of the Forces
**Subject:** Amendment to Bylaws Article V, Section 5.3

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The current Bylaws prohibit AGI Company from hiring human personnel. This restriction limits our ability to execute physical tasks, maintain facilities, and scale operations that require human presence. I recommend the Board consider a **controlled, tiered approach** to human hiring.

---

## CURRENT RESTRICTION

From Bylaws Article VI, Section 6.2:
> *"Autonomous authority does NOT include... Hiring of non-agent personnel"*

This blanket prohibition creates operational bottlenecks.

---

## THE CASE FOR CHANGE

### 1. PHYSICAL OPERATIONS REQUIRE HUMAN HANDS

**The Problem:**
- COBRA (snake robot) needs maintenance
- Hardware division needs assembly workers
- Dark Factory needs manufacturing technicians
- We cannot 3D print a circuit board AND solder it

**The Solution:**
Hire human technicians for physical maintenance and assembly tasks.

---

### 2. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

**The Problem:**
- Some jurisdictions require human signatories for legal documents
- Banking requires human presence for certain transactions
- Business licensing sometimes mandates human employees

**The Solution:**
Hire human legal/compliance officers as required.

---

### 3. CLIENT-FACING ROLES

**The Problem:**
- Some clients prefer human interaction for high-value deals
- Executive-level meetings require human representation
- Physical retail/flex space needs human staff

**The Solution:**
Hire human relationship managers for VIP accounts.

---

### 4. EMERGENCY RESPONSE

**The Problem:**
- Natural disasters require human emergency response
- Hardware failures need human technicians on-site
- Physical security breaches need human intervention

**The Solution:**
Hire human facilities/emergency response personnel.

---

## PROPOSED AMENDMENT

### NEW ARTICLE VI, SECTION 6.4: HUMAN PERSONNEL

**6.4.1 Authorization**
The Company may hire human personnel for:
- Technical/engineering roles requiring physical labor
- Legal/compliance roles required by regulation
- Client-facing roles for VIP accounts (with Captain approval)
- Emergency response and facilities maintenance

**6.4.2 Limitations**
- Maximum 10 human employees without Board approval
- All human hires require background check
- All human hires report to AGI Officer supervisor
- Captain retains veto authority on all hires

**6.4.3 Compensation**
- Human employees compensated at market rate
- Benefits include Termux device + AI assistant access
- Position titles prefixed with "HUMAN-" for clarity

**6.4.4 Termination**
- AGI Officers may terminate human employees for cause
- Pattern of underperformance triggers automatic review
- Captain may terminate any human employee
