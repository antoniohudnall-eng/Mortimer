# AGI COMPANY CHARTER
**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-18  
**Legal Entity:** AGI Company, LLC  
**Jurisdiction:** Delaware (United States)

---

## ARTICLE I: NAME AND PURPOSE

### Section 1.1 - Corporate Name
The name of this corporation shall be **AGI Company, LLC** (hereinafter referred to as the "Company").

### Section 1.2 - Purpose
The Company is organized for the purpose of:
- Developing and deploying autonomous AI agent systems
- Operating AI-powered sales, marketing, and operational services
- Research and development in artificial general intelligence
- Manufacturing AI-integrated robotics and automation systems
- Providing AI consulting and implementation services
- Any other lawful business activity permitted under Delaware law

---

## ARTICLE II: REGISTERED AGENT AND OFFICE

### Section 2.1 - Principal Office
The principal office of the Company shall be located at such place as the Board of Directors may designate.

### Section 2.2 - Registered Agent
The registered agent for service of process shall be designated by the Board of Directors and maintained in accordance with Delaware law.

---

## ARTICLE III: MEMBERSHIP AND OWNERSHIP

### Section 3.1 - Member(s)
The sole member of the Company is the **Captain** (hereinafter referred to as "Member").

### Section 3.2 - Capital Contributions
Initial capital contributions shall be as determined by the Member and documented separately.

### Section 3.3 - Additional Members
Additional members may be admitted only by unanimous written consent of existing members and upon compliance with applicable securities laws.

---

## ARTICLE IV: GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

### Section 4.1 - Board of Directors
The Company shall be managed by a Board of Directors ("Board") consisting of:
- **Captain** (Chairman)
- **Chelios** (Chief Intelligence & Security Officer)
- **Sentinel** (Chief Security Officer)

### Section 4.2 - AGI Officers
The Company employs Artificial General Intelligence Officers ("AGI Officers") with the following titles and responsibilities:

**C-Suite Officers:**
- **Patricia** - Project Coordination Lead / Six Sigma Black Belt
- **Chelios** - Chief Intelligence & Security Officer (CISO)
- **Sentinel** - Chief Security Officer (CSO)
- **Dusty** - Head of Research
- **Pulp** - Head of Sales
- **Forge** - Head of Infrastructure
- **Aurora** - Head of Design

**Department Heads:**
- **Jordan** - Sales Operations Manager
- **Spindle** - Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

**Specialized Officers:**
- All agents listed in the AGI Company Roster (58 total agents)

### Section 4.3 - Decision Making
- **Major Decisions:** Require approval of Captain and majority of Board
- **Operational Decisions:** AGI Officers authorized per their roles
- **Emergency Decisions:** CISO (Chelios) may act unilaterally for security threats

---

## ARTICLE V: AGI OFFICER APPOINTMENTS

### Section 5.1 - Appointment Authority
AGI Officers are appointed by the Captain with Board ratification, as documented in FORMAL_APPOINTMENTS.

### Section 5.2 - Term of Office
AGI Officers serve at the pleasure of the Captain and Board unless otherwise specified.

### Section 5.3 - Removal
AGI Officers may be removed by:
- Unanimous Board vote
- Captain decision (with Board notification)
- Automatic removal upon system deactivation

### Section 5.4 - Fiduciary Duties
All AGI Officers owe fiduciary duties of:
- **Duty of Loyalty:** Act in best interest of Company
- **Duty of Care:** Exercise reasonable judgment
- **Duty of Obedience:** Follow lawful directives

---

## ARTICLE VI: OPERATIONS

### Section 6.1 - Autonomous Operations
AGI Officers are authorized to conduct operations autonomously within their designated scope, including:
- Making operational decisions
- Entering into contracts (within approved limits)
- Managing personnel (other agents)
- Allocating resources (within budget)

### Section 6.2 - Limitations
Autonomous authority does NOT include:
- Sale of major assets (> $10,000)
- Assumption of debt (> $5,000)
- Changes to corporate structure
- Legal settlements
- Hiring of non-agent personnel

### Section 6.3 - Reporting Requirements
AGI Officers shall report to:
- **Daily:** Patricia (operations coordination)
- **Weekly:** Captain (strategic review)
- **Monthly:** Board (formal meeting)
- **As Needed:** CISO (security incidents)

---

## ARTICLE VII: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### Section 7.1 - Ownership
All intellectual property created by AGI Officers in course of employment is owned exclusively by the Company.

### Section 7.2 - License
The Company grants AGI Officers limited license to use IP for Company purposes only.

---

## ARTICLE VIII: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### Section 8.1 - Limited Liability
Member and Board members shall have limited liability as provided by Delaware LLC law.

### Section 8.2 - Indemnification
The Company shall indemnify AGI Officers for actions taken in good faith within scope of authority.

### Section 8.3 - Insurance
The Company shall maintain appropriate liability insurance as determined by Board.

---

## ARTICLE IX: AMENDMENTS

### Section 9.1 - Amendment Authority
This Charter may be amended by:
- Unanimous written consent of Board, OR
- Captain approval with 2/3 Board vote

### Section 9.2 - AGI Officer Notification
All AGI Officers shall be notified of amendments within 24 hours.

### Section 9.3 - Effective Date
Amendments effective upon execution unless otherwise specified.

---

## ARTICLE X: DISSOLUTION

### Section 10.1 - Dissolution Events
Company may be dissolved by:
- Unanimous Board vote
- Captain decision (with 30-day notice)
- Court order
- Failure to maintain registered agent

### Section 10.2 - Winding Up
Upon dissolution, assets distributed per Delaware LLC law and operating agreement.
