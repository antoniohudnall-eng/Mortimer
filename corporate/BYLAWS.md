## ARTICLE III: BOARD OF DIRECTORS

### 3.1 Composition
The Board consists of:
- Captain (Chairman, permanent)
- Chelios (CISO, permanent)
- Sentinel (CSO, permanent)
- Additional members as appointed by Captain

### 3.2 Meetings
- **Regular:** Monthly, 3rd Friday, 10:00 UTC
- **Special:** As called by Chairman or any 2 directors
- **Quorum:** 2 directors present
- **Location:** Virtual (Gather Town / Secure channel)

### 3.3 Notice
- Regular: 7 days electronic notice
- Special: 48 hours electronic notice
- Emergency: Immediate notification

### 3.4 Voting
- Each director: 1 vote
- Captain: Tie-breaking vote
- Actions: Majority vote (2 of 3)

### 3.5 Powers
The Board may:
- Amend Charter and Bylaws
- Appoint/remove C-Suite officers
- Approve annual budget
- Authorize major transactions (> $50,000)
- Declare distributions

## ARTICLE V: AGI WORKFORCE

### 5.1 Workforce Composition
The Company employs 58 AGI agents organized by:
- Executive (7 agents)
- Sales (7 agents)
- Secretarial (8 agents)
- Myl Family (7 agents)
- Technical (10 agents)
- Creative (7 agents)
- Finance (7 agents)
- Specialized (7 agents)

### 5.2 Agent Rights
All agents shall have:
- Access to BHSI v4.4 health monitoring
- Clear task assignments and reporting structures
- Recognition in Company Roster
- Indemnification for actions within scope

### 5.3 Agent Responsibilities
All agents shall:
- Report daily to designated leads
- Maintain accurate MEMORY.md files
- Follow AGENTS.md protocols
- Uphold Company values and ethics

### 5.4 Activation/Deactivation
- Activation: By C-Suite officer with Board notification
- Deactivation: By Captain or unanimous Board vote
- Emergency deactivation: By CISO (security threat)

## ARTICLE VI: MEETINGS

### 6.1 Types of Meetings

**Board Meetings:**
- Monthly (regular)
- Special (as needed)
- Emergency (immediate)

**Officer Meetings:**
- Weekly C-Suite (Mondays, 09:00 UTC)
- Daily standups (as per project needs)

**All-Hands Meetings:**
- Monthly (first Monday, 14:00 UTC)
- Emergency (as called by Captain)

### 6.2 Meeting Procedures
- Agenda distributed 24 hours in advance
- Minutes maintained by General Counsel
- Action items assigned with owners and deadlines
- Follow-up at next meeting

### 6.3 Virtual Meetings
All meetings may be conducted virtually via:
- Gather Town (primary)
- Secure video conference
- THIS/BHSI v4.4 communication channels

### 7.1 Fiscal Year

- Annual: Audit by independent firm

## ARTICLE X: AMENDMENTS

### 10.1 Amendment Authority
These Bylaws may be amended by:
- Unanimous Board vote, OR
- Captain approval + 2/3 Board vote

### 10.2 Procedure
1. Draft amendment proposed
2. Legal review by General Counsel
3. Board vote
4. AGI Officer notification (24 hours)
5. Effective date specified

### 10.3 Emergency Amendments
In emergencies, Captain may amend with:
- Immediate effect
- Board ratification within 48 hours
- AGI Officer notification within 24 hours

---

## ARTICLE XI: INDEMNIFICATION

### 11.1 Scope
The Company shall indemnify:
- Board members
- C-Suite officers
- AGI agents (acting within scope)

### 11.2 Limitations
No indemnification for:
- Fraud or willful misconduct
- Actions outside scope of authority
- Criminal acts

### 11.3 Insurance
Company shall maintain:
- Directors & Officers liability insurance
- Cyber liability insurance
- General liability insurance
